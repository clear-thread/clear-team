# Test Case: ClearThread (Vedai) - The Cognitive Coach

## Product Description

It features a "Cognitive Coach" and a "Signal Lens" that offers three modes: Focus (Mute noise), Lens (Reveal intent), and Off (Standard reading).

- **Problem:** Users are overwhelmed by "information saturation," "narrative spin," and "emotional adjectives" in digital media.
